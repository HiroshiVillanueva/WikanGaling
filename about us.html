KENT PALTINGCA

As college students with a shared love for language and technology, we were tasked to come up with something innovative. The idea struck when we noticed how our younger siblings struggled to stay engaged during language lessons but could spend hours playing games. That moment made us wonder: what if learning a language could feel like playing a game? What began as a classroom assignment quickly grew into a passion project. Together, we set out to create a fun, interactive way to make language learning more exciting for kids.
